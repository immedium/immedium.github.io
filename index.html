Brief

Immedium is an independent Research & Entrepreneurship lab devoted to the mission of helping people catch their dreams. We work with innovators in science and technology to discover and craft new forms of intelligence for the formulation and resolution of their hardest problems. We build systems of safe AI for self-actualization, and perceptive tools for collective thought and action.

Products

Immedium is weaving a web of interdependent tools. We currently operate in stealth mode and demo our technology to select investors and supporters. Areas of focus include: AI safety, creativity, and discovery.

Services

Immedium's offering covers:
  • Building bespoke engines and tools to solve unique problems.
  • Deriving tailor-made models and solvers.
  • Constructing on-demand software systems.
  • Sparring on strategic and organizational design.

Systems

Beyond the universal loop of Perception, Thought and Action, we strive for:
  • The unification of two complementary perspectives into a single fundamental system:
    • The primal: the discoveries of scientists, engineers, designers and artists.
    • The dual: new discoveries using AI, data science, formal methods, games and UX.
  • An improvisational programming model that:
    • Combines the best of fundamental research and entrepreneurial ingenuity.
    • Facilitates the dynamic multidisciplinarity of a mixed mathematical art.
    • Allows a symbiosis between man and machine, to materialize a cyborg mind.
  • An abundance of alternate visions, because we recognize that perception is a creative process.
